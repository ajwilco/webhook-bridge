Suspension
A Webhook Bridge

About

Suspension connects one service to another by making their webhooks compatible.

It acts as a bridge between the two services by unpacking the the data from the source hook and repackaging it before resending it to the destination hook.

Currently, the only hooks supported out of the box are the outgoing Prodpad webhook and the incoming Slack webhook.

Suspension is under active development and looking for the next bridge to build.

Got a suggestion? Get in touch.

Use

Suspension connects the outgoing webhook of one service to the incoming webhook of the other when the format of payload is incompatible.

Currently, the only webhooks supported out of the box are the outgoing Prodpad webhook and the incoming Slack webhook.

  1. Add an incoming webkook in Slack or find an existing one.
  2. Copy your Unique Webhook URL - it should look something like this: 
    https://your-team.slack.com/services/hooks/incoming-webhook?token=6dr1...
  3. Paste your unique webhook url into the form below.
  4. Add your email address to be notified when the api is going to change (spam free).
  5. Go to Prodpad and add a Custom Webhook from the Integrations & API section
  6. Paste your bridge webhook url into the webhook url field in prodpad (Give it any name you like while you are there).
  7. You are good to go! Click Push idea to... to push to slack.
